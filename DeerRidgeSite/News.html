News

Nations Cup

Tuesday, May 6 2014

This past weekend Megan Lane competed San D'Or on the Canada Team 1 for the Nations Cup that took place at Global dressage festival. Alongside teammates Belinda Trussel, Brittany Fraser and Chris van Martels, the group earned a combined score of 204. to place them on the podium for in the silver medal. On Thursday, Megan and San D'Or were 5th in the class having recieved over 68% in the Intermediaire 1 to qualify for the individual Freestyle the following day.


San D'or Earns Top Results in Season Debut

Tuesday, May 6 2014

Deer Ridge's San D'Or earns top results in the CDI Gold Coast Opener! On Saturday Megan and San D'or earned a score of 69.649% against an experienced field of competitors. The pair competed in the Intermediare 1 the following day to a score of 70.263%. and were named small tour champions. Despite his young age, he demonstrates a superb disposition in coping with the electric atmosphere in the show ring. Since importing San D'Or as a four-year-old from Holland, we have very pleased with the development of this talented horse and look forward to his continued progression through the FEI levels.


DR's Trainer takes 1st place in Grand Prix Special!

Tuesday, May 6 2014

On January 11 Deer Ridge Equestrian's trainer Megan Lane rode Caravella to a score of 68.98% in the Grand Prix Special. The result earned the pair a first place finish in only their second CDI this year. Last year Deer Ridge opted to remain in Canada to train and is being rewarded as this show marks a competitive return to Wellington's show circuit. Next weekend Megan will return to the World Equestrian Festival show grounds to compete Caravella in the large tour and Deer Ridge's San D'or in the small tour.


Florida Bound!

Tuesday, May 6 2014

This winter, Megan Lane & five Deer Ridge Equestrian horses will spend the season training and competing on the FEI Florida circuit. We are fortunate to return to the beautiful Stillpoint Farms in order to train with Oded Shimoni. With the magnitude of competitors concentrated in Wellington and WEG just around the corner, we are looking forward to a exciting 2014 season!


Clinic with Oded Shimoni

Tuesday, May 6 2014

Deer Ridge Equestrian was excited to welcome Oded Shimoni for a clinic this past week! Oded has represented Israel at the World Equestrian Games, has qualified for the Olympics, and has trained numerous horses to the Grand Prix level. His keen eye and assured approach provided valuable advice as Deer Ridge's talented string of horses progress through the FEI levels. This was a great prelude to the Canadian competition season and Deer Ridge would like to thank Oded for a wonderful clinic and all the riders who attended!
